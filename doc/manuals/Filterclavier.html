Filterclavier

Functionality

Filterclavier is a Filter whose frequency is set via MIDI input.

Tips

Controls

  • Transpose: Transpose the incoming MIDI signal by this amount of half tones
  • Detune: Detune the filters frequency by this amount of cents of a half tone
  • Mode: Choose the type of the filter
  • Max. Resonance: Add an accentuation to the center frequency. Used on a bandpass the surrounding frequencies are decreased in level and the band gets more narrow, a bandreject is narrowed down to a thin needle
  • Portamento Time: Smoothen your automated jumps between frequencies
