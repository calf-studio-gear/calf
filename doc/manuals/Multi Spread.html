Multi Spread

Functionality

This tool spreads (mono) signals to stereo by distributing the frequency range equally to the left and right channel. The frequency range is split into four bands each of which have its own amount knob. Choose the amount of filters per band with the "Filters" knob.

Tips

Use this tool to enhance old mono recordings or to give some stereo depth to instruments which are recorded in mono.

Be careful with the sub band! Normally the bass of any instrument is best located in the center of the mix. Better use the mid and the high band to distribute the signal since those are the ones mainly responsible for the stereo impression.

Controls

  • Bypass: Don't process anything, just bypass the signal
  • Input: Control the input level before the signal is processed
  • In L/In R: The level after the input knob
  • 0dB: Flashes if input level rises above 0dB
  • Output: Control the output of the plugin - clipping could destroy your signal
  • Out L/Out R: The level after processing and after the output knob
  • 0dB: Flashes, if the signal rises about 0dB in the output stage
  • Sub Amount of the distribution in the sub band
  • Low Amount of the distribution in the low band
  • Mid Amount of the distribution in the mid band
  • High Amount of the distribution in the high band
  • Filters Choose the amount of filters per band
  • Mono In Enable to copy the left channel to the right on input
